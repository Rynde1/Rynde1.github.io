Introduction
The “See the Ability” advocacy highlights the
strengths and talents of individuals with
disabilities. It creates an inclusive platform
that shifts the focus away from limitations,
encouraging the community to recognize
and celebrate the abilities of everyone.
The goal is to empower PWDs to lead
fulfilling lives, achieve their goals, and
make meaningful contributions to society.

Benefit

The talent show fosters inclusion, raises
disability awareness, and empowers
participants by providing a platform for
creative expression. It also inspires the
community to rethink perceptions of
disability, promoting a stronger, more
inclusive society.
Call for Action
The campaign invites community
members to participate by attending,
supporting, or sharing the event. By
promoting the message of inclusion and
celebrating the talents of individuals with
disabilities, everyone can help create a
more accepting and accessible
community.
